We limit our data collection to only what is necessary to provide our DNS and VPN services. Specifically, we collect:

We do not collect personal identifiable information such as:

The information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To deliver our DNS and VPN services to your device effectively and securely.
  • Application Development: To improve, maintain, and optimize our application's performance, reliability, and user experience.
  • Troubleshooting: To identify and resolve technical issues that may affect the performance of our application.
  • Security: To protect our application and users from fraudulent or unauthorized access and activities.

4. DNS Services and Third-Party DNS Providers

Our application utilizes third-party DNS services (such as Google DNS and other public DNS providers) to resolve domain names. When you use our application:

  • DNS queries from your device are sent to these third-party DNS providers.
  • The DNS queries contain the domain names you are attempting to access, but not your personal information.
  • These providers may have their own privacy policies governing how they handle this information.
  • We do not share any additional user information with these DNS providers beyond what is necessary for the DNS resolution process.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party DNS providers if you have concerns about how they handle DNS queries:

  • Google DNS: https://developers.google.com/speed/public-dns/privacy
  • Other DNS providers used by our application may have their own privacy policies available on their respective websites.
